Vishakapatnam

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is the second biggest city in Andhra Pradesh. The city derived its name from God Visakha, the God of valor. Located 625 km from Hyderabad, Visakhapatnam is a major port on the South Eastern coast of the country and serves as the headquarters of the Eastern Naval Command.
